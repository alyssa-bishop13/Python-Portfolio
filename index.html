About

Hi, I’m Alyssa currently based in North Bay. I began my journey in health care, graduating from Georgian College (with honors) with a diploma in Opticianry and also passing the national exams (NACOR) in 2021. I am now studying at Algonquin College, aiming to blend my background in eye care with my growing skills in tech.

While I’ve always been passionate about the optical industry, I’ve also had a long-standing side hobby in building computers, playing video games, and figuring out how things work - and why. After working with various optical software platforms and ordering systems, I realized I wanted to dive deeper into the tech side of things.

I'm excited to continue learning, creating, and growing my portfolio!
